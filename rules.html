📘 How to Play

Guess the 5-letter word in 6 tries or fewer. Each guess gives feedback on how close you are:

  • 🟩 Green: Correct letter in the correct spot
  • 🟨 Yellow: Correct letter in the wrong spot
  • ⬜️ Gray: Letter not in the word

🟩 Regular Mode

The classic game. A new shared word is seeded every hour. Everyone gets the same challenge.

🧪 Corrupted Mode

Some tiles may lie. Gray tiles however cannot.. — trust nothing else. Beat the game by second-guessing the game.

📐 Blueprint Mode

You see the final AI guess and full board. Deduce the correct answer using logic and elimination.
